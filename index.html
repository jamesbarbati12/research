Framework

SCORING MODEL

Each report opens with three composite scores — Risk, Growth Potential, and Overall — built from a weighted blend of growth trajectory, financial health, and valuation discipline.

Traditional screener ranges (P/E, P/S, EV/EBITDA) are applied but not treated as automatic disqualifiers: a pre-profitability or premium-multiple name can still score well if growth and balance-sheet quality justify the premium. The goal is a repeatable, honest framework — not a model that only ever says buy.
